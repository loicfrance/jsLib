game.objectProperties.energy

Source: game2/object_mixins.js:90

provides energy methods and properties. adds the energy attribute to the object, with a default value of 0 added as a prototype property, and a maxEnergy optional attribute (not created by default, but used in method if it exists). also adds getters and setters for those two attributes, and two other methods : recoverEnergy and useEnergy
