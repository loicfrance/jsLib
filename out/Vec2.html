sets angle of this vector to the given one (in radians) : x= cos(a)*magnitude, y= sin(a)*magnitude

angle  number

angle of this Vec2, computed from the coordinates : atan2(y, x).

magnitude  number

magnitude of this Vec2, calculated from the coordinates : √(x2 + y2).
