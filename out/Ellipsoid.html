area  number

area of the ellipsoid : radiusX * radiusY * π

excentricity  number

excentricity = (focus distance) / (horizontal radius)

read-only

focusDistance  number

focus distance : √(radiusX2 - radiusY2)

perimeter  number

approximation of the perimeter of the ellipsoid : π * √(2 * (radiusX2 + radiusY2))

read-only

squareFocusDistance  number

square of the focus distance : radiusX2 - radiusY2
