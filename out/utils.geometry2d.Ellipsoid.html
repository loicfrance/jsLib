utils.geometry2d.Ellipsoid

Source: utils/geometry2d.js:1630

a shape representing an ellipsoid, optimized for drawing. make sure to always have radiusX ≥ radiusX for the methods to work properly. You can reorder radiusX and radiusY by calling the {@utils.geometry2d.Ellipsoid#checkRadius|checkRadius} method. ⚠ ellipsoids cannot be used for collision detection, and most of their methods take time. You can make an ellipsoid-like Polygon by calling the method toPolygon, or directly by calling the static method Polygon.createEllipsoid.
